If you want to be computer engineer,you should study at least 4 years in college.
In first class,you receive basic engineering courses,such as mathematics,physics.
What does a computer engineer do?
In fact a computer engineer is a designer because they do computer design.
Computer engineers embed computers in other machines and systems and they improve new system for everybody.
Computer engineers usually have training in electronic engineering,software design and hardware-software integration instead of only software engineering or electronic engineering.
Computer science always evolves so computer engineering is always important.
Work
You can find a work easily at the end of the school, for example; you can work in banks,software companies,research-development companies,etc.
