 <div id="style4">In first class,you receive basic engineering courses,such as mathematics,physics.</div
 ><div id="style3">What does a computer engineer do?</div>
 <div id="style4">In fact a computer engineer is a designer because they do computer design.</div>
 <div id="style4">Computer engineers embed computers in other machines and systems and they improve new system for everybody.</div>
 <div id="style4">Computer engineers usually have training in electronic engineering,software design and hardware-software integration instead of only software engineering or electronic engineering.</div>
 <div id="style4">Computer science always evolves so computer engineering is always important.</div>
